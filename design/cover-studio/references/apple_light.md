# Apple-Light Visual Specification

## Palette (极致极简)
- **Background**: `#FAFAFA` (Soft White)
- **Primary**: `#0071E3` (Apple Blue)
- **Text (Primary)**: `#1D1D1F` (Charcoal Black)
- **Text (Secondary)**: `#86868B` (Neutral Gray)
- **Border**: `#D2D2D7` (Fine Line)

## Typography (优雅排版)
- **Font Stack**: `SF Pro Display, system-ui, sans-serif`
- **Title Weight**: 600 (Semibold)
- **Letter Spacing**: -0.5px for titles (Compact)

## Layout (非对称平衡)
- **Safe Area**: 10% Margin all around.
- **Lines**: Thin 1px solid lines with `#D2D2D7`.
- **Shapes**: 12px border-radius (Apple Rounded Corners).

## Negative Constraints
- No gradients with more than 2 colors.
- No heavy glows or shadows; use 1px stroke instead.
